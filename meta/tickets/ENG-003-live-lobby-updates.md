# ENG-003: Live Lobby Updates

## Goal
All players already in the lobby see new players appear in real time, without needing to refresh.

## Key Flows
- When a new player joins, the lobby on all connected devices updates immediately to show them
- Players are grouped by team and listed in join order
- Each player entry shows their name and word submission count (initially 0)

## User Verification
- Two devices are in the same game lobby; a third device joins → both existing devices show the new player instantly, grouped under their chosen team
